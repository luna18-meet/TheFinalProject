About The Website

Think Like A Manager is basically a website where it mainly focuses on management in different aspects: time management, management in general, and the student council.
Now you might ask yourself, what does the student council have to do with management? Well, as most of us might know, in every student council there is a president that is chosen by the students themselves. The council president should also be a manager and have some management skills so that the council would be a succsseful one.
And still, you might ask yourself, why do we need to know about the student council, or management in general? Imagine living a life without having the ability to manage your day, and everything you do is just unorganized, what a mess that would be, am I right?
On the other hand, managing your day, or even your life in a correct way is not easly done, it requires skills, smartness and sometimes experience. That is why this website is here for you!
It provides you information for everything you need to know about management.
