About The Website

Think Like A Manager is basically a website where it mainly focuses on the student council, it provides information on how the council's members are chosen, what happens during the meetings and it helps you know when there is a meeting in case you forgot.
In addition to the student council, the website also contains information about management in different aspects. You might ask yourself, what does the student council have to do with management? Well, you will find out the answer the more you go over the website.
Let us talk about the importance of management in our daily lives, most of the things that you face during each day, demand you to make a decision, and the proccess of making a decision - a wise one.
